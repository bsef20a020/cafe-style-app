OUR STORY

Slow mornings, cozy evenings.

NOFFELO is designed like a warm studio with espresso tones, cream highlights, and soft brass light. In the daytime café, guests enjoy carefully brewed coffee and matcha with a quiet work-friendly vibe. In the evening lounge, lighting softens for relaxed conversations, date nights, and intimate gatherings.
